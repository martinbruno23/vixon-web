04 — Neuro

Train the
Brain First.

Modern football is won in milliseconds. The difference between a good player and a great one isn't physical — it's cognitive. Vixon puts that on the training field.

01
Visual Stimulus

LEDs fire a pattern — a color, a sequence, a direction. The player can't anticipate it. Their brain has to process it in real time.

02
Cognitive Decision

In fractions of a second, the player reads the signal and decides: move left, pass, hold. The brain is the first muscle to train.

03
Physical Execution

The body acts. The vest captures timing, response patterns, and consistency — all in movement, not in a lab.

04
AI Lab Reports

After each session, the app shows cognitive performance data: reaction time, attention drops, decision accuracy. Real numbers, real progress.
